UX + Me, A Love Story

The moment I learned about UX was the moment I knew what I wanted to do with the rest of my life. It now has roots in every thing I do, from my work designing and redesigning web and mobile apps, to the way I approach and experience life.

How did this love affair come to be? Having lived in Germany and South Korea while growing up, I’ve always enjoyed studying cultures and learning how people work. (I received my BA in Anthropology from the University of Missouri in 2013.) Being the hands-on person I am, I needed a way to apply this passion to a practical field, where I could spend my time making things and making things better.

Enter UX, and queue heavenly chorus. The way that I can employ my skills and passions in one satisfying, practical career. Every day, I get to study and advocate for the user, strategize with businesses, craft beautiful, usable products, and then test them and make them even better. I couldn’t ask for more.
